How to Play Traffic Rider

Hit the road and weave through traffic in Traffic Rider, a high-speed motorcycle racing game where you're on the road for an endless road race. The faster you go, the bigger the rewards, so muster up some courage and overtake cars at speeds above 100 km/h to earn bonus points and extra time.

Complete daily rounds to earn in-game currency, which can be used for bike upgrades and new rides. Choose from multiple game modes, including Career, Endless, Time Trial, and Free Ride, each offering unique challenges.

Brace yourself for dynamic weather conditions—riding in the rain adds an extra layer of difficulty, so stay focused and don’t let the thunder throw you off!

Play it safe or push the limits—speeding past traffic earns more rewards, but be careful of oncoming cars! How far can you ride without crashing?
